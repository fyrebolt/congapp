Checklist

Before the election, you should...

Edit

Register to Vote

Make sure you're registered to vote well in advance of the election.

Research the Candidates

Learn about the candidates running for office, their platforms, and their positions on various issues.

Locate your Polling Place

Find out where you need to go to cast your vote.

Check Voter ID Requirements

Ensure you have the necessary identification or documents to vote, if required in your area.

Make a Plan

Decide when and how you will vote, whether it's on Election Day, during early voting, or by absentee ballot.

Encourage Others

Encourage friends and family to vote and provide information about the voting process.

Stay Informed

Keep up with any last-minute updates or changes related to the election.

Bring necessary items

On Election Day, bring any required identification, your voter registration card, and a list of your chosen candidates and positions on issues.
