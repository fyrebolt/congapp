For reference: Why should I vote?

  • Voting is a fundamental civic duty
  • It allows individuals to actively shape their government and have a say in decisions that affect their lives
  • Voting helps select representatives who align with one's values and beliefs
  • It influences critical policy decisions on various important issues
  • Voting fosters community engagement, awareness of current events, and public discourse
  • It serves as a check and balance mechanism to hold elected officials accountable for their actions and decisions
  • Voting promotes equality and inclusivity by granting all eligible citizens an equal voice in the political process
  • It is a powerful tool for advocating change and progress in society
