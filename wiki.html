The U.S. Governemnt attempts to adhere to constitutional principles of democracy. This means that we have a government for the people, and the best way for a country of a whopping 340 million people is for the people to cast a vote for a representative that shares the demands of the individual. Voting is the way our voices can be heard.

Voting is your opportunity to have a direct impact on the policies and decisions that shape your community and country. By casting your vote, you play a crucial role in preserving and strengthening the principles of democracy.

Many people are ignorant towards the significance of EVERY INDIVIDUAL VOTE. With this app, we hope to promote political representation awareness to guarantee that the government that controls OUR lives will listen to our demands and needs.
